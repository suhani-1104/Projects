About This Project

This is a project built by an AI student passionate about solving real-world challenges using machine learning. It uses LSTM to classify email messages based on meaning, not just keywords.

The goal is to protect inboxes with smarter filtering and help users stay focused and safe.

Why It Matters

Spam detection protects users from phishing, scams, and distractions. It’s crucial in today’s digital world.

Spam filters ensure secure communication and peace of mind—whether for personal or business use.
